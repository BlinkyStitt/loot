Loot Splitter
=============

Setup
-----

    $ . setup.source_me

Example
-------

    $ python loot.py --cp 100 --ep 0 --sp 700 --gp 2000 --pp 18 -n 4
        split 18 pp into 4 piles of 4 with 2 leftover...
        dividing 2 pp...
        dividing 1 pp...
        dividing 2000 gp...
        dividing 1999 gp...
        dividing 1998 gp...
        dividing 1997 gp...
        dividing 1996 gp...
        dividing 1995 gp...
        dividing 1994 gp...
        dividing 1993 gp...
        dividing 1992 gp...
        dividing 1991 gp...
        dividing 1990 gp...
        dividing 1989 gp...
        dividing 1988 gp...
        dividing 1987 gp...
        dividing 1986 gp...
        dividing 1985 gp...
        dividing 1984 gp...
        dividing 1983 gp...
        dividing 1982 gp...
        dividing 1981 gp...
        split 1980 gp into 4 piles of 495 with 0 leftover...
        split 700 sp into 4 piles of 175 with 0 leftover...
        split 100 cp into 4 piles of 25 with 0 leftover...

        The loot divided equally :)
        Character 0: {'cp': 25, 'ep': 0, 'gp': 495, 'pp': 5, 'sp': 175}
        Character 1: {'cp': 25, 'ep': 0, 'gp': 495, 'pp': 5, 'sp': 175}
